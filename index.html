About Me

Designing with

I'm Anvitaa Chopra — a multidisciplinary designer with a deep love for crafting meaningful, user-centered experiences. Currently pursuing my studies at NIFT, I blend creativity with strategy to bring ideas to life.

From product packaging to digital interfaces, I believe great design lives at the intersection of aesthetics and functionality. Every project is an opportunity to tell a story — whether through a minimalist UI, a bold brand identity, or an immersive product experience.

My approach? Research-driven, detail-obsessed, and always human-first. I thrive on solving complex design challenges and turning abstract concepts into tangible, beautiful solutions.
